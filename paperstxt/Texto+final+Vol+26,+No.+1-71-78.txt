ABSTRACT. In this work it is presented a mathematical algorithm in stages for the calculation of the
partial volume for the (cid:222) lling of horizontal tanks with inclined (cid:223) at heads and with its measuring
center separated from the geometrical one of the container. It is also intended to address this lack
in the Cuban norms.
This paper also shows the general steps for obtaining the value of uncertainty of the calibration with
a coverage factor 2 and then the result could be inserted to the national chain of traceability of the
magnitude Volume and Expense.
