Abstract We present a hybrid algorithm that combines a global strategy of optimization with a Branch and
Bound method based on semi-defined positive relaxation for the calculation of an ε-aproximated solution of
boundconstrainednonconvexquadraticprograms. Therealizedexperimentsprovethattheproposedalgorithm
decreasesthenumberofnecessaryrepetitions,thenumberofexplorednodesandtimeofexecutionascompared
withotherstandardglobaloptimizationmethodsforthisproblem.
