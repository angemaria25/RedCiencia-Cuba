many applications to the functions which can be represented as Walsh series have been found, making the
study of such system very important. In this article, several structural properties of the algebra of functions
withabsolutelyconvergentWalshseriesareproved. Inparticular,isarguedthatthespectrumofthisalgebrais
aregularmetricspacehomeomorphetothedyadicgroup. Alsoispresentedthedifficultproblemofspectral
synthesis.
